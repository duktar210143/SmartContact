Welcome to smart contact

smart contact manager application is designed to help users efficiently manage their contacts. It will offer a range of features to simplify the process of adding, updating, and organizing contact information. Users will be able to easily search and filter their contacts, as well as add notes and tags to individual contacts for better organization.

What will be included

We are working on scaling this system, we will include search of contact and also work on building better and interactive UI .
